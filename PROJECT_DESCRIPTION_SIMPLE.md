# OrbitOPS - Space Traffic Control

Think of this project as a **"Traffic Control System for Space."**

Just as air traffic controllers watch airplanes to prevent crashes and guide them safely, OrbitOPS watches over the thousands of satellites and pieces of "space junk" floating around our planet.

## What it does (Plain English):

1.  **3D Space Map:** It shows a beautiful, video-game-like globe where you can see exactly where every satellite and piece of debris is right now. It's like Google Earth, but for space.
2.  **Collision Warning:** It acts like a safety alarm. If a piece of space junk is getting too close to an important satellite (like the ones that provide your GPS or Internet), the system predicts the danger and warns the operators so they can move the satellite out of the way.
3.  **Launch Planner:** When rocket scientists want to launch a new rocket, this tool helps them find a clear path through the crowd of objects already up there, ensuring a safe journey to orbit.
4.  **Real-Time Tracking:** It constantly updates the positions of everything, tracking over 14,000 objects as they zoom around Earth at thousands of miles per hour.

## Why it matters:
Space is getting crowded! With more companies launching satellites every month, the risk of collisions is growing. If two things hit each other in space, they shatter into thousands of smaller pieces, creating a cloud of bullets that can destroy other satellites. OrbitOPS helps prevent this chain reaction and keeps space safe for everyone.
